Our Story

Welcome

Our cafeteria was founded in 2019, and working to this day to provide Astana IT University’s students and stuff with a high-flying culinary experience made of sharing in a warm and human atmosphere conducive to beautiful encounters. The kitchen offers its spectacular scrolls, you can see the ballet of the cooks, busy, concentrated. The gestures are precise, the cooking mastered, the time stopped. Everything agrees. However, as it is University cafeteria, the prices are humble and do not come as a regard to those, who want to enjoy the Kazakh cuisine and so special atmosphere. Furthermore, we highly value convenience. We have microwaves and kettles, which make warm food accessible to the clients and even allow them to bring their own food. Napkins, tableware and etc. are also fully accessible, as we believe that it is the key to the comfort and hearts of our students and staff.
